What is the Foundation Course?

The Foundation Course is a structured introduction to casual Japanese, designed to help you transition from correct speech to natural, human conversation.

This isn’t slang memorization or confidence tricks. It’s learning how Japanese is actually used between real people.

The goal is simple: stop sounding like a textbook, and start sounding like a person.
